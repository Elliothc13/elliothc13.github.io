Lilith - Queen of Demons.

Lilith

Lilith is a dangerous demon of the night, who steals babies in the darkness. Lilith may be linked in part to a historically earlier class of female demons. Lilith first appeared as a contrast to Eve. She was created from the same clay and at the same time as Adam. She was Adam's first wife. In the early Middle Ages Lilith became identified as the queen of Asmodeus, the king of Demons.
